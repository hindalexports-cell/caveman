```bash
curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/JuliusBrussee/caveman/main/install.sh | bash
```

## How Haroon Uses Caveman

### Activate
```
/caveman          → standard caveman (recommended for daily use)
/caveman lite     → slightly shorter, still professional
/caveman ultra    → maximum compression, very fast
```

### Normal Claude vs Caveman Claude for Trade Queries

**Normal Claude (slow, long):**
> "Based on current market conditions and taking into account various
> factors including seasonal demand patterns and freight availability,
> I would suggest that the optimal export price for Kesar mango would
> be approximately $1.85 per kilogram on FOB Mundra basis..."

**Caveman Claude (fast, direct):**
> "Kesar mango FOB Mundra: $1.85/Kg. Margin 18%. Check Talala APMC rate first."

**Same answer. 80% less words. Haroon save time.**

## Compress Your Business Files (Save 46% Tokens Every Session)

Caveman can compress your CLAUDE.md or context files:
```bash
/caveman:compress HINDAL_CONTEXT.md
```
This cuts input tokens by ~46% every time Claude reads your business context.
For Haroon, that means faster responses on EVERY query, every day.

## Caveman-Commit (For Git)
